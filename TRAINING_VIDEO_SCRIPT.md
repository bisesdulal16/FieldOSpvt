# FieldOS — Training Video Script

A ready-to-record screen-recording script for the pilot institution's staff. Record two short
videos — one for **field officers** (phone), one for **branch managers** (dashboard). Narrate in
**Nepali** for field staff; English or Nepali for managers. Keep each under ~6 minutes.

**How to record:** screen-record the phone (scrcpy/QuickTime for the officer video) and the
dashboard browser (the manager video). Follow the `[SHOW] / [SAY] / [DO]` beats. Text in **bold**
is what appears on screen; *italic* is the spoken narration.

Demo login for recording: officer `FO-208 / 1234`, manager `BM-001 / 1234`. **Tell trainees these
are demo credentials — in real use they get their own Staff ID and PIN.**

# VIDEO 1 — For Field Officers (record on the phone, ~5 min)

### Scene 1 — Welcome (0:00–0:30)
- [SHOW] The app login screen with your institution's logo.
- [SAY] *"This is FieldOS — your daily work app. It replaces the paper register and the constant
  phone calls. It works even when there's no internet. Let me show you a normal day."*

### Scene 2 — Log in (0:30–1:00)
- [DO] Type Staff ID, type the 4-digit PIN, tap **Login**.
- [SAY] *"Enter your own Staff ID and PIN — never share your PIN. This is like your signature; every
  action you take is recorded under your name."*

### Scene 3 — Start your day (1:00–2:00)
- [DO] On Home, tap **Start Day**. Allow the camera. Take a selfie. Confirm.
- [SHOW] The "Day started — Verified at the branch office" message.
- [SAY] *"At the start of the day, at the branch, tap Start Day and take a quick photo. The app
  checks you are on the office Wi-Fi. This is your attendance — no separate register. You only
  need to do this once, at the office, in the morning."*
- [SAY, note] *"If you're not on the office network it won't let you start — that's expected."*

### Scene 4 — See your work (2:00–2:40)
- [DO] Tap the **Tasks** tab. Scroll the client list.
- [SAY] *"Here are today's due clients, sorted for you — the most urgent and overdue at the top.
  The red 'NPA risk' ones need a visit. No more guessing who to see first."*

### Scene 5 — Record a collection (2:40–3:50)
- [DO] Tap **Collect** on a client. Show "Location captured." Tap **Set Full Due Amount** (or type
  the amount on the keypad). Choose **Cash**. Tap **Record Collection**.
- [SHOW] The digital receipt (amount, client, your name, remaining due, GPS logged).
- [SAY] *"Enter exactly what the client paid, choose how they paid, and tap Record. You get a
  digital receipt instantly. Very important: the system automatically sends the client an SMS with
  this exact amount. So always enter the true amount — the client will see it on their phone."*
- [DO] Tap **Share Receipt** to show the share sheet (optional).

### Scene 6 — Visit check-in & Promise-to-Pay (3:50–4:20)
- [DO] From a client, tap **Visit**. Show GPS captured. Pick an outcome (Collect / Promise-to-Pay /
  No payment).
- [SAY] *"When you visit a client, tap Visit — it records where and when. If they can't pay today,
  tap Promise-to-Pay and set a date; the app reminds you. A visit doesn't have to be a payment."*

### Scene 7 — Works offline (4:20–4:50)
- [SAY] *"In areas with no signal, keep working — everything saves on your phone and syncs
  automatically when you get signal again. You'll see 'Pending Sync' until it uploads. Nothing is
  lost."*

### Scene 8 — End the day (4:50–5:00)
- [DO] Show the End-of-Day summary briefly.
- [SAY] *"At day's end, submit your End-of-Day summary and log out. That's your whole day — faster
  than paper, and your manager already has everything."*

# VIDEO 2 — For Branch Managers (record on the dashboard, ~5 min)

### Scene 1 — Why this replaces phone calls (0:00–0:30)
- [SHOW] The dashboard login with the institution logo.
- [SAY] *"This is your branch dashboard. Instead of calling officers all day, you see what's
  happening live. Let me show you the parts you'll use every day."*

### Scene 2 — Log in & Branch Overview (0:30–1:15)
- [DO] Log in. Land on **Branch Overview**.
- [SAY] *"At a glance: who started their day, collections received today, visits done, PAR
  follow-ups due, and exceptions. These update as your officers work."*

### Scene 3 — Day-Start Attendance (1:15–2:00)
- [DO] Open **Day-Start Attendance**.
- [SAY] *"Every officer's start-of-day shows here with their selfie and an 'Office network' check —
  proof they were physically at the branch. No more guessing who came in and when."*

### Scene 4 — Staff Map (2:00–2:45)
- [DO] Open **Staff Map**. Point to the two officers' markers and trails.
- [SAY] *"This shows where each officer was last seen, from their real visits and collections — not
  constant tracking, only official actions. You can see coverage across your centers at a glance."*

### Scene 5 — The trust feature: Client Receipts (2:45–3:45)
- [DO] Open **Client Receipts**. Point to the messages sent.
- [SAY] *"This is the important one. Every collection automatically sends the client an SMS with
  the exact amount the officer recorded. The officer cannot change or skip it. If an officer ever
  records less than a client paid, the client sees the wrong amount and calls you. This is how you
  stop collection leakage."*

### Scene 6 — Verify the work (3:45–4:20)
- [DO] Quickly show **Collection Progress**, **PAR / Overdue Follow-up**, **Audit Logs**.
- [SAY] *"Collections by officer, overdue clients to chase, and a full audit log — every sensitive
  action tied to a named person and time. When compliance asks 'who did this and when', it's here."*

### Scene 7 — Approve loans (4:20–4:50)
- [DO] Open **Loan Approvals**. Approve a pending application, then Disburse.
- [SAY] *"When an officer submits a loan application from the field, it appears here. You approve
  and disburse — officers cannot; only managers can. Disbursing sets up the repayment schedule
  automatically."*

### Scene 8 — Assign & close (4:50–5:00)
- [DO] Show **Assign Task**.
- [SAY] *"Assign work to officers here, and they see it on their phones instantly. That's your day —
  full visibility, less phone-tag, and proof for every rupee."*

## Trainer notes (not narrated)

- Record after you've loaded the institution's real branding + a few real clients, so trainees see
  their own names and logo — it lands better than demo data.
- For field officers, do a **hands-on session** after the video: have each officer start their day,
  record one real collection to a test borrower (their own phone number), and watch the SMS arrive.
  Seeing the SMS land is the moment they understand it.
- Emphasize three habits: **enter the true amount**, **start day at the office**, **keep working
  offline**. Everything else is intuitive.
- Keep a one-page printed cheat-sheet in Nepali at each branch for the first two weeks.
